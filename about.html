JEN BAEK

she/her

I was born in Atlanta, Georgia and raised in Montgomery, Alabama. When I was a kid, I used to draw epic scenes with dinosaurs riding motorcycles and wielding flaming katanas. And...now I'm here!

Drawing + writing are my main hobbies. I have much more experience in drawing than writing, but both bring me joy and have also landed me some pretty fun gigs.

Q&A

I currently don't take personal commissions, but I'm open to projects from institutions, companies, and organizations. Please email me with details if you'd like to collaborate!

Q: What drawing software do you use?
Procreate on iPad Pro

Q: What other platforms do you use that I can follow?
To be honest, I get a little intimidated by posting my art on social media. I know, bummer.
If I ever work up the courage (or if it becomes a necessity), I may post on Tumblr or Instagram and link those accounts on this site.
